Tours

There are two types of tours we provide, which are half-day tours and day tours. Schools and communities are welcome to book our service and tour itineraries can be arranged if required. For any specific service, we encourage you to call +853 000 000 0000 or email nomuchemail@newgrange.ie.

Overview

Join us to learn the history of Newgrange, Dowth and Knowth! Our half-day and day tours take you into the principal monuments in the World Heritage Property in Ireland. All these sites have been built for over 5000 years and they attract a great amount of tourists to visit every year.
As the official authority for Newgrange and Knowth, we will introduce you to walk around Newgrange, Dowth and Knowth with the best guide. The chamber visiting is a must when visiting us. Exploring these buildings with a mysterious vibe is a great adventure that should not be missed.
Our guides will lead you through an informative day with the history of our sites along with some fascinating tales that you will never know by yourself. Our tours will be both educational and entertaining. One thing for sure is that all of our staff is professional and qualified.
You will never regret joining our tours! It will be an unbelievable “time travel” when we dig into history by exploring Newgrange! We treasure your experience and time, therefore, we will depart at 8:00 am with a full and fruitful schedule and return to Dublin by 1:00 pm or 6:00 pm depends on your tour.
As for the full-day tour, we aim to provide you with more than knowledge. You will visit the local community in Drogheda to not only learn what forms this city but also enjoy the historical and warm atmosphere of the city.
They will be the tours that offer historical enrichment to you once you join them. We are all looking forward to seeing you.

Half-Day Tour Itinerary

08;00 AM: Depart Dublin
We meet at the front entrance of Trinity College Dublin. You will board our coach with a bottle of water and a booklet of the itinerary of the tour. You can meet your fellow traveller and relax when you get on our coach. However, we will not wait for any late customer and a refund is not possible in this case. Therefore, we suggest you arrive 15 minutes earlier and pay attention to any phone call.

08:45 AM: The visitor Centre Exhibition
This is the starting point for the day. In the exhibition, we will introduce you to history, myth and folklore. Here, we will also remind our tourists about some notices and prohibitions for Newgrange, Dowth and Knowth. If you do not obey any prohibitions and rules as mentioned, you will face a fine, penalty or sentence for prison. We appreciate our tourists, and we hope that we can enjoy the tour together and also help the maintenance of these lovely sites.

10:00 AM: The Dowth
Seeing is believing, therefore, after learning the history in the exhibition, we will walk across the River Boyne to Dowth. Having watched the X-ray map in the exhibition, tourists will now resonate with the site better now. They will be amazed and wonder what the reason behind Dowth is. We are happy to take pictures for our tourists and answer any question.

11:00 AM: Newgrange
This is one of the main sites of our tour because of the well-preserved heritage and tourists are allowed to enter the chamber to feel the wisdom of our precedence. It is not a secret that the chamber’s design is meticulous and divine that the passageway will be lighted up by a window in the morning during the winter solstice period. We are very delighted if we share our passion and knowledge with our tourists, and it will be a highlight of the tour.

Noon: Knowth
You will not be disappointed even though you visit Newgrange before Knowth. There are 21 tombs in Knowth and all of them create a mysterious feeling for us and you might wonder what every tomb was for. We will guide you to explore them and encourage you to imagine. We ensure that you will love Knowth as much as Newgrange!

1:00 PM Depart Drogheda to Dublin
You will be back in Dublin by 14:00.
